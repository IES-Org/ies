Overview

Welcome to the Information Exchange Standard. This collection of RDF ontologies provides a standardised 4D ontology and various domain extensions.

Available Ontologies

IES Common

Foundational 4D ontology concepts and shared vocabulary that forms the core of the Information Exchange Standard.

IES Built Environment

An IES extension ontology for concepts describing the built environment domain.

IES National Security & Defence

An IES extension ontology for describing concepts in the national security & defence domain.

IES People

An IES extension ontology for describing concepts about people, organizations, roles, and responsibilities.

IES Systems

An IES extension ontology for describing complex systems, components, their relationships, and interactions.
